Klein Curaçao

Klein Curaçao is indeed a small, deserted island off the coast of Curaçao. But with its many pearly white beaches with palm trees and turquoise water, it is heaven to relax. You go there on a luxury yacht for a day of snorkeling with turtles, sunbathing under the palapas with a delicious BBQ for dessert. Don't forget your sunglasses on this trip because the beaches are really dazzling. You sail in one and a half hours to your destination on this day: Klein Curaçao. You wake up with a cup of coffee or tea. With a bit of luck you can see dolphins as the boat dances over the waves. And once you've put your feet firmly in the sand, grab a snack before exploring the island. Order product
